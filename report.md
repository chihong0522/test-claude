## Category-Level Risk Factors

1. **Unlock overhang dominates 2024-cohort tokens.** EIGEN, ETHFI, ENA, ONDO, STRK, ZK, REZ, PUFFER, SWELL, OBOL, MORPHO all have meaningful 2026 cliffs. Prefer fully-unlocked names (UNI, AAVE ~96%, LDO, PENDLE, ENS, VIRTUAL) unless the unlock is already priced in.
2. **Governance-only tokens = value trap.** ARB, OP, STRK, ZK, Ondo (pre-switch), COMP all fit. Require a specific fee-switch/buyback catalyst to invest.
3. **Post-Dencun L2 economics.** Sequencer revenue collapsed ~90%. L2 tokens need a non-fee story (ENS = revenue; TAIKO = narrative; MNT = treasury).
4. **Stablecoin regulation.** GENIUS/STABLE-style US framework and MiCA enforcement affect ENA/SKY/USUAL/ONDO differently — monitor H2 2026 legislative calendar.
5. **AI-agent narrative cyclicality.** VIRTUAL/AIXBT/GAME are high-beta to narrative return. Size accordingly.
